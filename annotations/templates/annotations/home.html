Let's build the epistemic web, together.

The concept of the epistemic web was first introduced by Malcolm Hyman and JÃ¼rgen Renn as a new chapter in the history of knowledge representation. While the semantic web represents knowledge as a static graph of concepts and propositions, the epistemic web encodes the multi-dimensional contexts in which those concepts and propositions emerge: the texts that embed them, the times, places, and contexts of interpretation that constitute local forms and moments of knowledge and knowledge-making.
